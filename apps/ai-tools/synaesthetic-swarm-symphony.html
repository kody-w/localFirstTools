Welcome to Synaesthetic Swarm Symphony

A collaborative instrument where swarm agents paint with sound and compose with color. Drawing creates music, music creates visuals, and swarms self-organize with emergent behaviors.

How to Use

  • Draw on Canvas: Click and drag to spawn swarm particles that follow boids flocking behavior
  • Colors = Sounds: Each color produces a different musical frequency
  • Swarm DNA: Adjust separation, alignment, and cohesion to change swarm behavior
  • Acoustic Architecture: Enable effect zones where shapes modify audio passing through them
  • P2P Multiplayer: Create or join a room to collaborate with others in real-time

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Space - Spawn swarm at cursor position
  • R - Toggle recording
  • C - Clear canvas
  • H - Toggle this help overlay
  • D - Toggle left sidebar (Swarm DNA)
  • E - Toggle right sidebar (Effects)
  • M - Toggle microphone input
  • 1-7 - Select color palette

Emergence Music

The swarm behavior generates music through these mappings:

  • Density: More particles = louder volume
  • Alignment: Aligned swarms = consonant harmonies
  • Separation: Scattered swarms = dissonant notes
  • Position: Swarm center maps to frequency spectrum
